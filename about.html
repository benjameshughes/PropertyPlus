About Us

Get to know us, we don't bite

Property Plus has grown from a single 3 bedroom house into a 70+ room private landlord/agency in just a few short years. Having grown purely on profit, hardwork, and determination we can offer the best and most affordable rooms to live

Based in Corby, Northamptonshire, we have amased 14 properties and 70+ rooms all in the town of Corby. With all our properties either located in the town centre or just a bus ride away from the industrial estates you'll find the perfect room to comfortably live in.

Being a private landlord/agency we are able to offer the best in terms in prices, accomodation, and help throughout the entire renting process. We make it simple, quick, and easy to view a room and you could be in a room within as little as one hour! Being a private landlord you know who you are renting from and you can easily get the support you need if anything should arrive and we'll strive to fix the issue within days not weeks. No more jumping through hoops!

We are also multilingual! Understanding someone shouldn't be a chore and shouldn't cause any problems which is why we employee people of all nationalities and backgrounds. Our three main languages are: English, Romanian, and Russian. So if you want you can speak in a language you are comfortable with.

We pride ourselves on satisfaction and comfort.
